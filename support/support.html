Support & Help Center

📖 Getting Started

Welcome to My Stars! Our app is designed for parents and guardians to help children learn daily routines and tasks through a visual, step-by-step system with rewards.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and follow the onboarding process. You'll need to:

  • Enter your name and email address
  • Confirm you are 18 years or older (required for parental accounts)
  • Create a secure password
  • Set up a PIN for admin access
  • Add your child's profile and select tasks

Why do I need to confirm I'm 18 or older?

My Stars is designed for parents and guardians to set up and manage task tracking for their children. The 18+ requirement ensures that only adults can create accounts and configure app settings, preventing children from overriding parental controls.

How do I create a task?

From the parent dashboard (accessible with your PIN), tap the "+" button to create a new task. You can customize:

  • Task title and category
  • Individual steps with star rewards
  • Icons and colors
  • Time blocks (Morning, Afternoon, Evening)
  • Optional timers for each task

How do stars and rewards work?

Children earn stars by completing task steps. Stars can be spent to unlock rewards. Each reward has a star cost, and interactive rewards (like bubble animations) are unlocked daily to encourage continued engagement.

How do I add photos to activities?

When logging an activity or completing a task, tap the camera icon to either:

  • Take a new photo with your device's camera
  • Choose an existing photo from your library

Note for iPad users: Make sure to allow camera and photo library permissions when prompted.

How do I manage multiple children?

From the parent dashboard, you can add multiple child profiles. Each child has their own tasks, rewards, and star balance. Switch between children using the profile selector at the top of the main screen.

What is the PIN for?

The PIN protects parent/admin settings. Children can use the app to complete tasks and earn rewards, but they cannot modify tasks, change settings, or access sensitive features without the PIN.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the app and unlock admin access with your PIN
  2. Go to Settings → Account Settings
  3. Scroll to the "Danger Zone" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion

Warning: This action is permanent and will delete all your data, including parent profile, children profiles, tasks, rewards, and completion history.

Can I export my child's progress?

Yes! From the parent dashboard, access the completion history to view detailed records of all completed tasks. You can view progress over different time periods.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your data is stored securely on your device with encryption. We use:

  • Secure PIN storage with device-level encryption
  • Data integrity verification
  • Biometric authentication support (Face ID/Touch ID)

We do not collect or transmit your personal information to external servers without your explicit consent.

🛡️ Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the best experience:

  • Parent name and email for account creation
  • Task and reward data for app functionality
  • Completion history for progress tracking

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy in the app.

🆘 Troubleshooting

App won't load or crashes

  • Make sure you have the latest version installed
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check for iOS/iPadOS updates

Can't add photos on iPad

  • Go to Settings → Privacy → Photos
  • Make sure My Stars has permission to access photos
  • Go to Settings → Privacy → Camera
  • Make sure My Stars has camera permission

Forgot my PIN

  • If you have biometric authentication enabled, use Face ID or Touch ID
  • If you cannot access your account, you may need to reset the app (this will delete all data)

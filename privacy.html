Privacy Policy for My Stars - Task Tracker

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Overview

My Stars - Task Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app collects, uses, and shares your information.

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your tasks and to-do items
  • Task completion history and progress
  • App preferences and settings
  • Images you add to tasks (stored locally)

Third-Party Services

My Stars integrates with the following third-party services that may collect or process data:

1. AI Chat Features (Premium)

When you use the AI chat feature, your messages and task-related data are sent to our AI service provider (Anthropic Claude) for processing.

  • What is sent: Your chat messages, task descriptions, and related context you choose to share with the AI
  • Purpose: To provide AI-powered assistance with task management and insights
  • Data retention: We do not store your AI conversations; they are processed in real-time
  • Third-party policy: Anthropic Privacy Policy

2. Payment Processing (Stripe)

When you purchase premium features, payment information is handled by Stripe, our payment processor.

  • What is collected: Payment card information, billing details, transaction data
  • Purpose: To process your payment and provide premium features
  • Storage: All payment data is stored and processed by Stripe; we do not store your payment card details
  • Third-party policy: Stripe Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

  • Local data: Used solely on your device to provide task management functionality
  • AI interactions: Sent to Anthropic Claude only when you actively use AI features
  • Payment information: Processed by Stripe to complete transactions and manage subscriptions

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or behavior tracking
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not access your tasks unless you explicitly send them via AI chat

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local data is stored securely on your device using encryption
  • AI interactions are transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
  • Payment processing is handled by PCI-compliant Stripe infrastructure
  • We recommend enabling device security features (passcode, biometric authentication)

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible to you
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device
  • Control AI usage: AI features are optional; you can choose not to use them
  • Cancel subscription: You can cancel your premium subscription at any time through your App Store account

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general task management and is suitable for all ages.

Premium Features

Premium features (AI Chat and Insights) are optional upgrades:

  • Free version operates entirely offline with no data transmission
  • Premium AI features require internet connection and data transmission to AI provider
  • You can use the core app functionality without ever transmitting data externally

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

  • Email: support@mystars.app
